# RecruitAI

> AI-powered hiring decision platform.

RecruitAI is an intelligent recruitment platform that helps companies hire the right people faster while helping candidates discover the best career opportunities through AI-powered matching and resume analysis.

—

## 🎯 Mission

Our mission is to reduce hiring time, improve hiring quality, and make recruitment decisions smarter through Artificial Intelligence.

—

## ✨ Features

### For Companies

- Create and manage job postings
- Review candidates efficiently
- AI-powered candidate ranking
- Explainable hiring recommendations
- Recruitment analytics

### For Candidates

- Create a professional profile
- Upload resumes
- AI resume analysis
- Smart job recommendations
- Track job applications

—

## 🧠 AI Capabilities

- Resume Parsing
- Skill Extraction
- Semantic Matching
- Candidate Ranking
- Explainable AI
- Career Recommendations

—

## 🏗 Architecture

RecruitAI is designed using a modular architecture based on business domains.

Core domains:

- Identity
- Company
- Recruitment
- Candidate Intelligence
- Decision Engine
- Platform Services
